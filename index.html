Making Popcorn on a Stovetop

Popcorn in 10 easy steps.

Here's How:

1. Heat a spoonful of oil at a medium temperature in a large, deep pot.

2. Add 3-4 popcorn kernels, cover the pot, and wait for them to pop.

3. Add the rest of the kernels to the pot. Half a cup of popcorn kernels makes approximately 10 cups of popcorn.

4. Remove from the heating element. Wait 30 seconds and then return to heat. Make sure to keep the lid slightly ajar so that steam can escape.

5. Let the popcorn pop. Give the pot a gentle shake every now and then.

6. Once the popping slows, remove from the heat and uncover the pot.

7. Pour the popcorn into a bowl.

8. Return the pot to the heat and melt butter inside it.

9. Once the butter is melted, remove from the heat and drizzle onto the popcorn in the bowl.

10. Add salt to taste, mix, and enjoy!
