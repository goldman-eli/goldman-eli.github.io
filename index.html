To make popcorn on a stovetop:

  1. Melt a spoon full of coconut oil in a large, deep pot at a medium temperature.

  2. Add 3-4 popcorn kernels and wait for them to pop.

  3. Add the rest of the kernels to the bowl. Half a cup of popcorn kernels makes approximately 10 cups of popcorn.

  4. Cover the pot and remove from the heating element.

  5. Wait 30 seconds and then return to heat. Keep the lid ajar so that the steam can escape.

  6. Let the popcorn pop. Give the pot a gentle shake every now and then.

  7. Once the popping slows, remove from the heat and uncover the pot.

  8. Pour the popcorn into a bowl.

  9. Return the pot to the heat and melt butter inside it.

  10. Once the butter is melted, remove from the heat and drizzle onto the popcorn in the bowl.

  11. Add salt to bowl, mix, and enjoy!
